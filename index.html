Hi, I'm Josh - a software developer living in Berlin, originally from Canberra, Australia. I have a passion for technology and human- machine interaction. In my free time I like to sing karaoke, dance salsa and play volleyball.

I've been working as a software developer professionally for over nine years.

I've been facinated with computer programming since I was a kid, when I would spend enormous amounts of time learning about and tinkering with programming projects.

I first visited Berlin in 2015 and decided to move here shortly after. I currently live in Prenzlauer Berg.

I grew up in country Australia before moving to Canberra to study and begin work as a software developer.

I am currently attending a full-time intensive German language course.

Employment/Education

I have worked for several companies as a software developer, both in Australia as well as Germany. My knowledge and expertise is full-stack, covering backend systems to user-interface design. I have a special interest in user-interaction.

From June 2016 to December 2018 I worked for KIWI.KI GmbH as a full-stack developer. At KIWI I was responsible for a major overhaul of the KIWI Portal app, the primary interface for business customers into KIWI.KI systems.

From September 2012 to December 2015 I worked for General Dynamics Mediaware.

From January 2009 to August 2012 I worked for NCH Software. Immediately after completing my studies at the AIE, I was hired by NCH software.
